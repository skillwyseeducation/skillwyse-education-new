About Us

Skillwyse Education offers in-person AP Physics 1 classes, 90 minutes per week. Each class is followed by homework assignments of practice questions, including multiple-choice questions (MCQs) and free-response questions (FRQs). Our instructors also grade these assignments and provide feedback. We are one of the very few providers of in-person classes specifically designed to prepare students for the College Board AP Physics 1 and AP Physics C Mechanics test. Our classes are held in small batches of up to 8 students to ensure a personalized learning experience. .
