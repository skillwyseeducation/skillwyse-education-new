About Us

At Skillwyse Education, we specialize in in-person AP Physics 1 and AP Physics C Mechanics classes—designed from the ground up to mirror the rigor and style of the College Board exams. Our mission is to turn physics from “just another subject” into a source of genuine curiosity and confidence.

Expert-Led Instruction

Our instructors are deeply experienced in both teaching and grading AP Physics. They know exactly what College Board looks for—and how to instill those skills in you.

Intimate Class Sizes

With no more than 8 students per cohort, you get the personalized attention you deserve. Questions never go unanswered, and concepts are explored until they click.

Focused, Efficient Lessons

Each week you’ll dive into 90 minutes of high-impact learning: clarifying tough concepts, working through strategic problem-solving techniques, and reinforcing fundamentals.
